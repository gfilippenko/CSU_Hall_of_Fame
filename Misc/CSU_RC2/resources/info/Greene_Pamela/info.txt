Pamela Greene is among one of the earliest and finest female track athletes in school history, still holding records at CSU more than 35 years after she competed. Greene continues to hold the school record in the 220-yard indoor, the 220-yard outdoor, 100-yard outdoor and is third in the 50-yard indoor competitions. Possibly the greatest overall sprinter in school history, Pam Greene received the All-American Award for the 200-meter in both 1973 and 1976. She competed in the 200-meter at the 1972 Olympics while still attending high school at Denver Manuel. In 1973 she won the AIAW championship in the 200-meter competition.
